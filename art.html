ART

One of the first pioneers in body painting, Jacob Alexander aka the artist Zander consulted for Game Show Network's Skin Wars three years in a row, body painted for international acts such as Paul Van Dyk, Benny Benassi, Red Bull, Summerset Music & Camping Festival, Sexapalooza, as well as the "Party for a Purpose" movement with his band The Band Famous including the "Live + Follow Your Dreams" Benefit Music Festival in Jan. 2020 that supported CFF and My Friend's Place helping homeless youth. Zander makes art from mixed media to music, music videos, and more and is known for his signature body painting Ala "X Fashion", "Edible Art", and that his band The Band Famous is the first band in the world to self-create and develop an interactive app album for their debut album release! Learn about his past creative ambitions and ventures in this free documentary, "All Over the Walls" at http://karnakgallery.com
