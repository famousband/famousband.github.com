FAMOUS

About

fa·mous

adjective

1. A revolving door of talented artists & musicians under the leadership of singer & lyricist, Norell, Famous is constructed of lyrical content & improv sound.

2. Famous is composed of artists and musicians who are also developers and thereby developing musicians.

3. All of us, across the galaxy - we’re all stars.

4. The sounds of Famous can best be described as soul EDM, as well as experimental, electronic, and pop, with occasional visitations of acoustic melodies and haunting a cappella.

5. Famous welcomes collaborators of just about any walk of life. Got something special you'd like to share? Contact us if you're interested in affiliating with the Famous brand. We will always give credit to any artists, musicians, and otherwise, who extend their creative talents for collaboration in any way.
