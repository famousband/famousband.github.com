"Live + Follow Your Dreams"

We rescheduled the "LIVE" benefit show due to weather and illness, bringing you not only a benefit concert, a good show for a great cause, but we doubled up, inviting you to "FOLLOW YOUR DREAMS" as well.

We enjoyed a magical weekend of live music featuring performances by Nur-D, Dakotah Faye, Korynn OC, DRYX, Quaesar, and The Band Famous. We honored and remembered our beloved friend Eyva Kid Cash, and raised awareness and funds for Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, as well as for My Friend's Place, helping homeless youth.

We had Pokémon Go YouTube celebrities PkmnMaster Holly, Trnr Tips, Mystic7, DarkMatterWolf, and more special guests that joined us for the first-ever live-streamed Pokémon Go PvP Ranked Tournament for Charity on Friday, Jan. 17th. Our event on Jan. 17th was an all ages event, and our event on Jan. 18th was 18+ with live music, body painting, and more. We even had live stand-up comedy on Jan. 18th courtesy of Braxton Herda, whom you may have seen in the movies "Long Shot", or "Arrested Development". We had PkmnMaster Holly as our official MC and co-host for the entire weekend! Gabriel Diggs,
who as a producer as well as DJ, has remixed highly acclaimed artists Idina Menzel, John Legend, Steve Aoki, Sarah McLachlan and Usher, and has DJed internationally in Ibiza, Brazil, Berlin, and more, was with us for the 17th and 18th of the "Live + Follow Your Dreams" Benefit Music Festival. We also had Moon Channel
and Power 106 and Young California's DJ Tee Time
DJing both nights!

As Always, for our long distance friends and fans:

We boadcasted the entire event - both nights - through the TBF apps; those with our app were able to tune in to the in-app concert if they were unable to be there in person.

Thank you to everyone in the vicinity of Los Angeles, California, who joined us for these monumental evenings helping two great causes.

Thank you for "Partying for a Purpose" with us!

Cheers,

The Band Famous®


Thank You for Supporting The Band Famous "Love Affair", event with American Heart Association as charitable beneficiary.

We invited you to join The Band Famous and friends on the evening of Friday, February 15, 2019. Be our Valentine!

We hosted a benefit concert featuring three wonderfully talented musicians and even better, wonderful humans. We brought you this love affair in partnership with the American Heart Association, which has been fighting against heart disease and stroke for over 100 years!

Being that heart disease is the number one killer worldwide, with stroke ranking in second place, we feel that this organization's work is vital.

The heart is the strongest muscle. We have demonstrated how strong our hearts are when we put our love to work collectively!


THANK YOU FOR HELPING US MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Thank you for joining us for the "Love Affair" benefit concert where we accepted donations on behalf of www.heart.org, and in addition 10% of proceeds from the event went to the Heart Association.

Thank You for Supporting The Band Famous "Follow Your Dreams",
event with LA Food Bank as charitable beneficiary.

We invited you to join The Band Famous and friends in a fundraising party on the evening of January 19, 2019. The theme: “Follow Your Dreams.” Being that January 15th is the birthday of one of the greatest activists for humanity, and Monday, January 21, 2019 was Martin Luther King Jr. day, we hosted a celebration of unity, and a celebration of the beauty that is the right to follow your dreams.

We believe that it is so important to follow your dreams that we want to show just how much of a positive effect we can make together.

Thank you for coming together with The Band Famous and friends where dreamers unite! 10% of proceeds from the event went to the LA Food Bank, out of deference to those at a station of life-struggles.

We believe everybody deserves the chance to create the life they want for themselves, and we know sometimes this is easier said than done. As brothers and sisters, we must band together to lift each other up and help one another persevere. Never give up. Follow your dreams and help make the world a better place to live in.


THANK YOU FOR HELPING US MAKE A DIFFERENCE

We hosted a food drive in partnership with the Los Angeles Regional Food Bank, which serves one million individuals, including 400,000 children, annually.

The LA Food Bank distributes food and other essentials to children, seniors, families and others in need.

We accepted donations to aid homeless youth, and had boxes for collecting donations of non-perishable food items that we delivered to the Los Angeles Regional Food Bank following this inspiring weekend.
