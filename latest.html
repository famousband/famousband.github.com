We will definitely make it a colorful weekend to remember raising awareness and proceeds for two causes that are close to our hearts: the Los Angeles LGBT Center, and the Los Angeles Animal Services.

01/19/2019 - The Band Famous "Follow Your Dreams",
event with LA Food Bank as charitable beneficiary.

Being that January 15th is the birthday of one of the greatest activists for humanity, and Monday, January 21st is Martin Luther King Jr. day, we wanted to host a celebration of unity, and a celebration of the beauty that is the right to follow your dreams.

We believe everybody deserves the chance to create the life they want for themselves, and we know sometimes this is easier said than done. As brothers and sisters, we must band together to lift each other up and help one another persevere. Never give up. Follow your dreams and help make the world a better place to live in.

We hosted a food drive in partnership with the Los Angeles Regional Food Bank, which serves one million individuals, including 400,000 children, annually.

The LA Food Bank distributes food and other essentials to children, seniors, families and others in need.

We accepted donations to aid homeless youth, and collected donations of non-perishable food items that we delivered to the Los Angeles Regional Food Bank.
