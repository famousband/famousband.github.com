Frequently Asked Questions

Well, hopefully you!
Also some very inspiring people including the co-creator of Adobe After Effects (think special FX in all your favorite movies) and the hover-board program, Greg Deocampo; or maybe you're familiar with the movie Guardians of the Galaxy?

Five Time Grammy Winning Artist BJ Thomas has one of his most famous songs in that movie's soundtrack. He downloaded our app and left a review for us on the iPhone App Store, and is connected to us on social media and very supportive.

+How do I submit?

It's simple. E-mail us via our Contact Page letting us know you want to submit your music to the Hall Of Fame.
You can even submit music that is not your own, provided you have the artist's permission. We are accepting original works only. No cover songs.

In your message to us, submit your preferred site/blog or virtual space you want us to include as your official site under our Hall Of Fame.
Include a YouTube video of a track or music video as well.
Any material will help us get a better idea of you and your vision that we may or may not pick to enter.

If selected to be added to the Hall of Fame, a simple waiver must be signed by the copyright holder of the submitted track(s).

+What do I get when I am entered?

Free promotion on our Hall Of Fame; regular inclusion in our monthly newsletters to our fan-base and audience; and shout outs on our social media.
Did we mention free?

You also get: A track included in our radio station which also includes some of our music exclusively available only on our station and not heard or found anywhere else on the Internet.

+What if I want to leave the Hall Of Fame?

We would be sad to see you leave our community and team but if you need to, we won't stop you!
We can also remove the track from the radio station with just a simple e-mail of your request.
