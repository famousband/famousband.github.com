Band Bio

The Band Famous came to life in 2013 when they filmed their own birth featuring spontaneous improvised music at the first meeting of founding members Zander and Norell with Terrance Clifford. Their debut album Last Words is an album of entirely improvised material.

What makes the Band Famous (TBF) different from other bands? 

  • They are arguably the first band in the world who can say one of their band members is a recognized body painter.
  • They are the first band in the world to release an album of completely improvised material.
  • They are the first band in the world to have their birth live streamed on the internet.
  • They are the first band in the world to self-release their debut album in the form of a “smart album”, their own virtual venue and record store.

TBF has proudly been endorsed by: Greg Deocampo, Atmosphere, Jack Ramos, Jay Gabler, Kim Dotcom, Emre Erturk, and Tom Green.

Photo by Jessica Ramaker Photography.

Lady Gaga and Björk are among entertainers that have an app, but they hired others to build it. Not TBF! They not only programmed and released the smart album, but they are also “fine-tuning” the new technology to cater to their fan base. In other words, the “downloadees” are going to determine the future of TBF’s apps.

They write all their own music, lyrics and do all of their own compositions and mastering - TBF is entertainment’s three-person army. The band formed over a spontaneous improv and livestreamed recording session in Saint Paul, MN, on July 26th, 2013.

Photo by Jessica Ramaker Photography.

From self-recording their debut album of complete improvisation, to programming their smart album and releasing it on the iPhone App Store and Android Google Play for free, TBF has proven to be a very capable group of pushing artistic and musical content, but is more focused on the art and the music than on distribution.

Photo/Style/Set/Makeup/Hair/Edit by Zander.

Their members include renowned artist and body painter, Zander, app expert Terry aka Terrance Clifford, who - also the creator of “TV Machines” - has over six apps on both iTunes and Google Play App Stores, and soulful singer and lyricist Norell, who is working on her first autobiography i.e. all three members are multifaceted artists and entrepreneurs.
