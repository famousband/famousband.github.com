SOUND & FX


Due to covid19 and many other reasons, The Band Famous enterprise is now completely green, solar-powered with a private water well on 41 acres in Arizona. Rather than renting, The Band Famous is now owning. We may have closed our live music venue in Los Angeles, but we still have all our of our equipment and are happy to offer competitive rates for sound equipment rental and other equipment rental.

CONTACT US

Let us know what your needs are for you event(s) or party and we will see how we are best fit to help accomodate your needs! We are open to renting out one JBL speaker, two JBL speakers, JBL speakers plus studio monitor and subwoofer, along with power amp, mixer, cabling, microphones, lighting, projector and other elements also available for rental. If you require technical support with any or all of the above setup and running at your event, we are open to work your event and setup and run everything, too! We have a cargo trailer we can travel with everything with a secure down payment in advance to our travels to you and your event(s)!
