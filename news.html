New EP To Be Frank:



Featuring Tracks 1. Golden Rule | 2. Space Jam | 3. Mermaid Energy | 4. On Top



To Be Frank is a four-track EP that is the 2.5 album release by The Band Famous. Nearly the entire project was completed solely by co-founder and lead singer-songwriter, Norell, with the exception of the final track, On Top, which was co-founder and former member Zander's contribution to the project. Norell contributed vocals, recording and mixing to Zander's song.





Present day, since May 2024, The Band Famous unique music project is a solo project now led by co-founding member, Norell.

The Band Famous (TBF) began as a husband/wife collaborative genre-bending music duo that formed in Minneapolis; software; and production space and venue formerly in Los Angeles, CA, and now upgraded to 41 acres in Arizona! Jacob Alexander Figueroa (Zander) and Mary Norell Jackson (Norell) originally made and ran the band, the software - The Band Famous website and apps on the iPhone App Store / Android Google Play Store - and formerly, the private venue in downtown Los Angeles, and in downtown Easat Los Angeles, CA. Now they work off-grid, fully solar powered on 41 acres in Arizona where they converted their energy once put into their “Party for a Purpose” themed Benefit Music Festivals and Events into more focus on animal rescue efforts.

Past benefit shows and festivals have aided LA Food Bank, American Heart Association, Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, and My Friend’s Place.
