Tweets by @TheBandFamous

December 2014:


Alas, our Kickstarter crowd funding project has drawn to an end. While we did not meet our funding goal in time, we'd like to thank all of our backers and supporters for trying to help us get there! Your love and support means the world to us.

On the bright side, we still succeeded in getting our music and our story out there, special thanks to Minnesota Public Radio, The Current, Local Current, Atmosphere, WeekONLatinoamerica, JCT TV, and every single one of you who shared the word in any way, shape or form.

We love and appreciate you all!

There are still so many exciting things to come! The smart album just may be a Famous exclusive. To all the Minneapolis/St. Paul family, the in-app concerts will still be coming to you, in addition we will keep working on porting our iPhone app to Android and releasing other exciting updates that you - the fans - can partake in.


That's right, our fans will MOLD the future of our app(s)...


It will take longer and will be more complicated due to current circumstances, but we will keep working with the same passion and diligence. We'll also finish our 2nd album and film a music video when time and funding does come into play.


If you are ever in need of affordable web and app solutions, please refer to phonixintl.com. By hiring us you will help support our music, art, and apps, too!

In other news, we are working on putting together a monthly music show at a fashion studio in Brooklyn, and NY/NJ friends, you can see Zander body painting live on Dec. 13th at Club Envy in NJ.


To our Minneapolis/St. Paul Famous Family, please check out our very talented bandmate Terry aka Jambots, multimedia artist and designer, for his art show in downtown Minneapolis. His art installation "TV Machines" is on display until March 2015 with MadeHereMN.org.

We appreciate your friendship, your encouragement, your teachings, and of course - the only reason we started it - you have to reach for your dreams. We'd like to extend our warmest holiday wishes to all of you!

We'll keep you updated with what life puts on our path.

Love,

The Band Famous

Oct/Nov 2014:


Please check out our Kickstarter:


Thank you to everyone who is able to donate and/or help spread the word. Sharing is caring!

We are The Band Famous; we are a group of artists, musicians, and developers. Every one of our members is doubly talented in music and development. Not only do we make the music, but also we bring it to you.

We gave you The Band Famous for iOS (iPhones, iPads, and iPod Touch) for FREE, which includes our debut EP, “Last Words”, videos, games, and so much more! We call it the smart album because it gives you our music, art, lyrics and more available offline forever once you download it to your phone! We have some exciting updates in development right now as well.

We are working to bring you The Band Famous for Android, and in addition to giving you our EP “Last Words”, we are also working on our second album “Awakening”.

With this Kickstarter, we will expand our platform from iOS to Android as well. One of our goals is to take our best single for our newest album “Awakening” and give you a high quality music video.

We also will need help with marketing and distribution on all of the above once we get there! We’ve developed what we feel is a very diverse reward system that is sure to have something for everyone! We’ve even set up some of the rewards to give you more than one option for whatever you feel comfortable donating to our cause.
