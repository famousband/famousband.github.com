December 2014:


Alas, our Kickstarter crowd funding project has drawn to an end. While we did not meet our funding goal in time, we'd like to thank all of our backers and supporters for trying to help us get there! Your love and support means the world to us.

On the bright side, we still succeeded in getting our music and our story out there, special thanks to Minnesota Public Radio, The Current, Local Current, Atmosphere, WeekONLatinoamerica, JCT TV, and every single one of you who shared the word in any way, shape or form.

We love and appreciate you all!

There are still so many exciting things to come! The smart album just may be a Famous exclusive. To all the Minneapolis/St. Paul family, the in-app concerts will still be coming to you, in addition we will keep working on porting our iPhone app to Android and releasing other exciting updates that you - the fans - can partake in.


That's right, our fans will MOLD the future of our app(s)...


It will take longer and will be more complicated due to current circumstances, but we will keep working with the same passion and diligence. We'll also finish our 2nd album and film a music video when time and funding does come into play.


If you are ever in need of affordable web and app solutions, please refer to phonixintl.com. By hiring us you will help support our music, art, and apps, too!

In other news, we are working on putting together a monthly music show at a fashion studio in Brooklyn, and NY/NJ friends, you can see Zander body painting live on Dec. 13th at Club Envy in NJ.


To our Minneapolis/St. Paul Famous Family, please check out our very talented bandmate Terry aka Jambots, multimedia artist and designer, for his art show in downtown Minneapolis. His art installation "TV Machines" is on display until March 2015 with MadeHereMN.org.

We appreciate your friendship, your encouragement, your teachings, and of course - the only reason we started it - you have to reach for your dreams. We'd like to extend our warmest holiday wishes to all of you!

We'll keep you updated with what life puts on our path.

Love,

FAMOUS



Norell

Born Mary Norell Jackson, and known also as simply Norell.

A multi-faceted artist, singer, writer, programmer, and communications enthusiast.

She studied Liberal Arts at Northland College for two years before transfering in 2009 and graduating with honors in 2011 from the University of Minnesota College of Liberal Arts, earning her Bachelor of Arts in Communications.

She tied for first place for best vocal performance at the CLA Talent Show in 2010. She sang with Terrell "Carnage" Woods from Rhymesayers entertainment, alongside Elliott Roche, who is now the frontman of the Cheap Thrills.

She also sang in Emily Colay's (Wookiefoot) choir, Cantera in 2011/2012. She has done backup vocals for the Artist Brant Kingman's daughter, alongside Emily Colay. She recorded vocals for MPLS.TV "Uptown Episode", performed with Elliott Roche (The Cheap Thrills) at Bubble Me, Driftwood Char Bar, and more, as well as performing solo at the Lounge Nightclub in Minneapolis at X Fashion on September 30th, 2011.

She has worked alongside the artist Zander on his body paint tours including Summer Set Music and Camping Festival, and Sexapalooza in Minneapolis and Portland in 2012, at Benny Benassi in 2013, followed by Gay Pride in Minneapolis at the Gay 90's.

She also conducted interviews, and put together several blogs for XTV (founded by Zander) in 2011, with highlights including interviewing Amy Klobuchar, Bill Moyers, and Jacob Frey.

She has been body painted live in the streets and is currently working on an autobiography, as well as an online gallery featuring her modeling ventures.

She is recording an album with Famous, and coding and programming regularly.

FAMOUS



Zander

Born Jacob Alexander Figueroa, but you know him as Jacob Alexander, as well as the Artist Xander/Zander.

Zander is a multimedia artist, with artworks featured in NY Arts Magazine, he is also a renowned body painter.

Zander’s body painting was featured at NY Fashion Week 2011, Summer Set Music and Camping Festival 2012, to high caliber shows such as Benny Benassi and Paul Van Dyk, to Black Kat Shows (Portland/Minneapolis), and most recently, MN Gay Pride 2013.

Published locally and internationally, Zander is a featured artist in "All Over the Walls", a Blue Bridge Media Group Documentary that premiered at the Minneapolis St Paul International Film Festival in 2011, as well as taking “Best Documentary Film” at the Highway 61 Festival that year, and also featured at the MCAD Underground Film Festival.

Zander’s artworks have been presented alongside Celebrity Designer Emre Erturk and fine artists Gigi Turker, and Gulay Alpay. Jacob Alexander is also proficient in violin, fluent in Spanish and an avid painter, and photographer. He helped develop Audrey Kitching's Crystal Cactus Product Web Page.

Currently pursuing his Bachelor of Science in Mobile Development.

Technology enthusiast. Front & Back End Developer.

When Zander isn't working on music with Famous, he is busy coding, or preparing for the next body painting exhibition. Stay tuned for more exciting things to come!
